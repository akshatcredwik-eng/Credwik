Yes, Credwik Free is genuinely free forever. No time limit, no credit card, no surprise charges. The free plan has limits (30 invoices/month, 50 parties) because we need you to experience the value before asking you to pay. When you hit those limits — and you will — upgrading to Solo at ₹299/month is a very easy decision.
Yes. Credwik supports Tally XML data import for Solo and Pro plans. You can migrate your party master, opening balances, and transaction history. We have a step-by-step guide that takes about 15 minutes. Your data in Tally is yours — we just make it easier to work with.
Yes. Credwik is built as a Progressive Web App with local SQLite caching on mobile. All reads work offline instantly. When you create a voucher offline, it queues and syncs the moment you reconnect. We know Indian internet can be unpredictable — especially in Tier-2 and Tier-3 cities.
Your data is encrypted at rest and in transit. Only you and users you explicitly add can access your data. We do not sell your data, share it with advertisers, or use it to train AI. You can export your entire data as Excel or PDF at any time. No lock-in, ever.

Support is available in English and Hindi via WhatsApp chat (all plans) and phone (Pro plan). Our help docs and YouTube tutorials are in Hinglish. We also have a community WhatsApp group with weekly GST tips and tax deadline reminders.
